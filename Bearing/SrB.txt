\\\SPHERICAL ROLLER BEARINGS///
Spherical bearings allow the angular rotation about to a center. Spherical bearings usually support a rotating shaft in the inner ring which is also moving in angular rotation motion
suitable for agricultural equipment, heavy duty & off -highway vehicles, construction and logging equipment.
spherical bearings are designed with 26 mm diameter to 24 mm.
